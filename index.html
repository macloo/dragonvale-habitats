Dragonvale Habitat Planner

Begin by choosing the Dragonvale habitat you want to populate. Only habitats that earn 1 million or more are listed here. Try out different combinations of dragons to discover how to get the most out of each habitat. Dragonvale is a mobile game. This app is not associated with the game or its parent company.

Note: For the Omnitat habitat, all dragons that earn more than 400 coins per minute at level 20 are listed.
